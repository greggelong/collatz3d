⚡ HOW TO PLAY (Best Strategy)

Your goal is to reach Room 1. Each door leads to a neighbor number. The fastest way to 1 is to follow this simple rule:

🚪 IF the number is EVEN → go to n ÷ 2 (half)
🚪 IF the number is ODD → go to 3 × n + 1

That’s the classic Collatz path. It always reaches 1.

📖 Example:
Start at 7 (odd) → click 22 (3×7+1)
Now at 22 (even) → click 11 (22÷2)
Continue: 11 → 34 → 17 → 52 → 26 → 13 → 40 → 20 → 10 → 5 → 16 → 8 → 4 → 2 → 1
Just follow the rule and you'll get there!

⚡ 游戏攻略（最佳策略）

你的目标是到达 房间 1。每个门通向一个相邻的数字。最快到达 1 的方法就是遵循这个简单规则：

🚪 如果数字是偶数 → 去 n ÷ 2（一半）
🚪 如果数字是奇数 → 去 3 × n + 1

这就是经典的考拉兹路径，它总是能到达 1。

📖 举例：
从 7（奇数）开始 → 点击 22 (3×7+1)
现在在 22（偶数）→ 点击 11 (22÷2)
继续：11 → 34 → 17 → 52 → 26 → 13 → 40 → 20 → 10 → 5 → 16 → 8 → 4 → 2 → 1
只要遵循这个规则，你就能到达！
